About ScCross

Welcome to the Cross-Species Single-Cell Transcriptomics Data Integration Benchmarking Platform

Our Platform

Our platform is dedicated to systematically evaluating and comparing cutting-edge methods for cross-species single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) data integration. Traditional studies have been limited by small-scale comparisons or single evaluation metrics.

To address these challenges, we have innovatively developed a multi-dimensional integration evaluation framework that systematically analyzes the impact of biological factors by controlling variables such as evolutionary distance, species number, and gene selection strategies. Through comprehensive comparison of 13 mainstream integration methods using 11 quantitative metrics, we reveal the relationship between integration performance and biological variables.
